Our Training Vision

Reginaldo Trust believes that skill development is the cornerstone of community empowerment. We provide hands-on training programs designed to equip individuals with practical skills for sustainable livelihoods.

From vocational courses to digital literacy workshops, our programs are tailored to meet the needs of the underserved communities across Goa — empowering them to build a brighter future.

Our Programs

Training Programs Offered

agriculture

Agricultural Training

Modern farming techniques, organic cultivation methods, and sustainable agriculture practices for local farmers.

computer

Digital Literacy

Computer basics, internet skills, and digital tools training to bridge the digital divide in rural communities.

handyman

Vocational Skills

Hands-on vocational training in trades like tailoring, plumbing, electrical work, and carpentry for self-employment.

health_and_safety

Health & Hygiene

Community health education, first aid training, and awareness workshops on hygiene and preventive healthcare.

eco

Environmental Awareness

Training on waste management, composting, tree plantation, and environmental conservation practices.

diversity_3

Women Empowerment

Self-help group training, entrepreneurship workshops, and financial literacy programs for women in the community.
