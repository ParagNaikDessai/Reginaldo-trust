About Us

Our Journey of Upliftment

REGINALDO TRUST is a registered trust working in the areas of upliftment of the poor, deserving and needy persons of society without discrimination of caste, creed or religion.

It is a pioneering institute which has been serving people for many years and has a vision to provide necessary support in all areas where its objectives are founded — serving communities across Curtorim village and surrounding areas for over 17 years.
