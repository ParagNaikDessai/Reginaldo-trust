Who We Are

REGINALDO TRUST is a registered trust working in the upliftment of the poor, deserving and needy without discrimination of caste, creed or religion.

It is a pioneering institute serving communities for many years with a vision to provide meaningful support across all areas of social development.

We have been environment-friendly for over 20 years, while formal structured programs began expanding more recently.
